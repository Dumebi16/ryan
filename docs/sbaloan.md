## SECTION 3 — WHAT IS AN SBA LOAN?
**Layout:** 2-column split (50/50). Left: eyebrow + headline. Right: body copy paragraphs.

**Eyebrow:** UNDERSTANDING SBA

**Headline:** What Is an SBA Loan?

**Body:**
An SBA loan is a small business loan partially guaranteed by the U.S. Small Business Administration. Because the government backs a portion of the loan, lenders can offer more favorable terms than you'd typically find at a traditional bank — lower down payments, longer repayment windows, and access to larger amounts of capital.

Whether you're looking to acquire a business, expand your operations, purchase equipment, or build your working capital, an SBA loan is one of the most powerful financing tools available to small business owners today.
