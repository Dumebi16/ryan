## The 4-Step Path to Funded
No mystery. No bureaucratic black hole. Here's exactly what happens:
**Step 1 — The Discovery Call (30 min)**
We talk about your business, your goals, and which loan type actually fits your situation. No fluff. Just a real conversation.
**Step 2 — Tax Return Review**
We pull your business and personal returns and figure out if you qualify. Honest assessment, no sugarcoating.
**Step 3 — Personal Financial Form**
We review your personal financial picture to confirm eligibility. If something's off, we tell you — and we tell you how to fix it.
**Step 4 — Financial Statement Review**
We verify the business can handle repayment. If you're ready, we move. If not, we map out what needs to happen.
Four steps. Clear answers. No wasted time on either side.
