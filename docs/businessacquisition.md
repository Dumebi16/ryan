## 5. SBA FINANCING BREAKDOWN

**Headline:**
Why SBA Financing Changes Everything

**Body:**
Most people assume buying a business means having millions in the bank. It doesn't.

With an **SBA 7(a) loan**, buyers can:
- Put down as little as **10%** of the purchase price
- Finance the rest over **10 years** at competitive rates
- Use the business's own cash flow to service the debt

**Example:**
A $500,000 business could be acquired with just $50,000 down. The business pays for itself.

> This is one of the most powerful — and underused — tools in business ownership.
