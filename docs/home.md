# Home Page — Ryan Kroge

## Purpose
Create a premium, conversion-focused homepage for Ryan Kroge that feels modern, editorial, and high-trust. The page should position Ryan as an SBA loan specialist and business acquisition consultant for serious business owners nationwide.

## Brand Direction
- Tone: confident, strategic, premium, approachable
- Personality: trusted advisor, clear guide, modern finance expert
- Visual style: cinematic black hero, bone-white content sections, gold luxury accents, restrained emerald support accents
- Design references: editorial fintech, boutique advisory, modern financial services

---

## Global Style System

### Typography
- **Primary font:** DM Sans
- **Secondary / branding font:** Inter styled like Kiona
- **Headlines:** DM Sans, medium to semibold, tight tracking
- **Body:** DM Sans, regular, relaxed line-height
- **Micro labels / nav / buttons:** Inter, uppercase, wide tracking

### Colors
- **Background / Black:** `#000000`
- **Primary Gold:** `#D4AF37`
- **Bone White:** `#F5F5F0`
- **Emerald Accent:** `#10B981`

### UI Principles
- Clean spacing
- Large type hierarchy
- Minimal borders
- Premium contrast
- Soft motion, never flashy
- Strong CTA clarity
- Image-led hero, content-led body

## Copy Guidelines
- Use concise, premium language
- Avoid generic phrases like “client satisfaction is my jam”
- Avoid sounding like a generic broker marketplace
- Prioritize clarity, credibility, and strategic tone
- Keep headings short and strong
- Body copy should feel calm, informed, and direct

## Design Guidelines for Antigravity
- Build the hero first and preserve the luxury tone across all sections
- Use dark/light contrast rhythm across the page
- Keep cards minimal and spacious
- Use gold sparingly for premium emphasis
- Use emerald only as a subtle support accent
- Favor clean motion: fade-up, stagger, scale-in
- Keep the layout responsive and elegant on mobile

## Conversion Priorities
1. Establish trust immediately
2. Explain what Ryan helps with
3. Show proof and experience
4. Make the process feel simple
5. Drive users toward consultation or application
